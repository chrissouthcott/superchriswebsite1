Chris and who shot the mayor part 2
If you didn’t read part 1 then here is a little overview: It was the mayors brothers birthday. Everyone in town went. Well at least they think everyone. When everyone was going inside to open the presents they heard a bang and saw the Mayor collapse into the punch bowl.

This time we start off at Mad Mans lab. He knows who did it. Mad man did not do it but is involved. Now wait and you’ll find out how it happened. We cross back to Super Chris who was visiting the mayor at hospital. The mayor could hardly speak. But Super Chris heard slightly, this: “a long lost enemy” “a lost enemy”. Super Chris was puzzled. The only long lost enemy he could think of was The Mad Scientist and he was dead!

Super Chris had a short sleep and then it hit him. He decided to call the whole town for a meeting.

Mean while in Mad Mans lab, Mad Man was chuckling to himself. In a black shadow something said “What a great plan Mad Man” Now Mad Man said” Oh I have missed you so much …”

The town meeting was in place. Super Chris walked to the microphone and told everyone what happened and finally this is what he said “I was a bit suspicious and then it hit me, today I went to The Mad Scientists grave and saw it was disturbed. When Mad Man was going to the toilet I snuck in to see a plan on the table. Next to the table was a test tube with ‘Mad Scientist DNA’ written on it . Then I quickly looked around to see a ‘person maker’ machine. It had a slot to put in DNA in and so then it hit me. He had gotten some DNA from the body of The Mad Scientists and brang him back to life and then told him to shoot the mayor!”

Later on that day the police arrested Mad Man and put him in jail. But the next day in the news Super Chris read ‘MAD MAN ESCAPED!’ but Super Chris will have to find Mad Man and The Mad Scientist next time.

The End
